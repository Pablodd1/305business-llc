## What 305business.LLC Actually Does (Legally)
1. **Ad space on website** — ✅ Legal (publisher/platform)
2. **Tools (search, alerts, analytics)** — ✅ Legal (SaaS)
3. **Monthly listing access** — ✅ Legal (subscription for visibility)
4. **Success/commission on sale** — ❌ NOT OFFERED (would be illegal)
5. **Negotiating deals** — ❌ NOT DONE (buyers/sellers connect directly)
6. **Representing parties** — ❌ NOT DONE (platform only)
